Jollof Rice

Ingredients

  • Curry powder
  • Rice (2 cups)
  • Water (2L)
  • Salt
  • Vegetable oil
  • Concentrated tomato paste
  • Powdered shrimp
  • Pepper
  • Black pepper
  • Paprika
  • Cabbage
  • Carrot
  • Fried chicken

Cooking Steps

  1. Heat vegetable oil in a pot over medium heat.

  2. Add tomato paste and fry until it darkens and the oil separates.

  3. Stir in curry powder, paprika, black pepper, powdered shrimp, and pepper for aroma.

  4. Pour in water and bring to a gentle boil.

  5. Add salt and adjust seasoning to taste.

  6. Rinse the rice thoroughly and add it to the boiling sauce.

  7. Stir, cover, and cook on low heat until the rice absorbs most of the liquid.

  8. Add chopped carrots and cabbage, mix lightly, and steam until tender.

  9. Fluff the rice with a fork to separate the grains.

Serving steps

  1. Scoop the jollof rice onto a plate or into a bowl, shaping it neatly.

  2. Arrange fried chicken pieces on top or beside the rice.

  3. Add Yebesse Fionfion(Spicy sauce)

  4. Enjoy!
